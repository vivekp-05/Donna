Under the hood

For the builders in the room.

Five agents on the job

  • Intake Parserany channel → a structured, multi-item donation
  • Offer Drafterwrites the pitch each pantry actually hears
  • Recipient Callervoice-calls pantries and community-agency leads
  • Manager Copilottune the whole system by chatting with it
  • Donor Callbackitemized "here's what we could and couldn't take"

The stack

  • VAPI + Twilioreal-time voice, inbound and outbound
  • Gemini 2.5 Flashagent brains · Deepgram transcription
  • Scoring enginedeterministic — no LLM touches allocation
  • Workers + D1runs at the edge; nothing lives on a laptop
  • MemoryStorepluggable — point it at your database with one class

Runs fully offline in mock mode — zero API keys, zero network calls. The entire demo scenario works on a plane.
